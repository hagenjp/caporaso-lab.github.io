We are a bioinformatics software engineering lab focused on developing applications of microbiome science in human health, cancer detection and treatment, and environmental sustainability.

We additionally lead activities focused on scientific computing education, bioinformatics education and training, and increasing diversity and inclusion in open source scientific software development.

Our software and educational content are free and open source, and we're committed to increasing accessibility and building community around reproducible bioinformatics and microbiome science.
